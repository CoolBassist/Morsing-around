This lesson we're going to work on the letters that are short. Here they are:

  • A .-
  • E .
  • I ..
  • M --
  • N -.
  • T -

Easy eh? Okay lets get to work.

1.

Lets start with the super simple letters. E is . and T is -. Im sure you can remember that.

Please translate the following, as good practice put a space between all letters so you can easily see the groups of dots and dashes.

EET

TTE

TET

ETE

For more practice make up your own exercises by writing out patterns of the letters you want to practice and translate them.

2.

Now we'll work on the letters that start with a dot. That is A .- and I ... Now translate the following:

TAT

EIT

TIA

AIE

TIE

Like before, make up your own combinations to translate to really solidify your knowledge.

3.

Now this is where starting to remember all the different combinations can be a little difficult. So if you get something wrong, the worst thing you can do is just stop. Keep doing dedicated practice. Dont advance if you can't remember a letter from the previous lesson. Take your time, make sure to internalise all the different patterns so you can recite them without even thinking.

Now we'll do the letters M -- and N -..

MIN

TAN

MAAM

IAN

ETIANM

MAN

If you can do all the letters here with no problem, next lesson away!
